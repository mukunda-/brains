Wordweb Privacy Policy

What information are you gathering from me?

We don't gather any information aside from what you normally input into our forms. Google may harvest some anonymous information for their analytics database.

What will you use my email for if I sign up with one?

We only use your email if you need to reset your password, and resetting your password is only possible if you use an email as your username.

Is my personal password safe with you?

Before your password is sent to us, it is hashed with a semi-secure method, as to mask it from any secret agents listening to your network traffic. When we receive it, it is hashed again using a highly secure method to store it in our database.
